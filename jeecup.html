About JEECUP

JEECUP is a state-level entrance exam for admission into polytechnic diploma courses in engineering, pharmacy, and other fields in Uttar Pradesh.

All Groups

Group A - Engineering
Group B - Agriculture
Group C - Fashion & Design
Group D - Management
Group E - Pharmacy
Group F - PG Diploma
Group G - Lateral Entry
Group I - Aircraft Maintenance

Eligibility

Engineering

10th pass with Science & Math (35%)

Pharmacy

12th with Physics, Chemistry & Biology/Math

Lateral Entry

12th Science or ITI pass
